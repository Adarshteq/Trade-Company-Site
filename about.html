Our Journey

Building Trust in Trading

2015

Founded with a Vision

Our journey began with three traders frustrated by opaque pricing and unfair practices in the industry. We set out to create a platform that would empower retail traders with institutional-grade tools.

2017

First Trading Signals Launched

We introduced our proprietary algorithm that analyzed market patterns with 85% accuracy, giving our members a significant edge in volatile markets.

2019

Mobile Platform Released

Recognizing the need for trading on-the-go, we developed our award-winning mobile app that brought professional tools to smartphones.

2022

Global Expansion

We expanded our services to 30 new countries and localized our platform in 12 languages, making professional trading accessible worldwide.

Today

Industry Leader

With over 150 employees across 5 continents, we continue to innovate while staying true to our founding principles of transparency and education.
